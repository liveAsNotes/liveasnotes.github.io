Project SSSXR

This project aims to build interoperable, measurable, and ethical XR spaces that improve our safety and possibility.
  • SSSXR.2019.F shows a fire evacuation situation in a building in VR.
  • SSSXR.2021.Q.MT1.Plus is a conceptual work to provide a VR/AR space that facilitates earthquake countermeasures by simulating them.
  • SSSXR.2022.P is under development that will provide a multiplayer experience of earthquake and fire to measure participants' behavior.
